Privacy Policy

The Sleep Diary uses a method called “local storage” to store and retrieve data. All data entered in this manner is securely stored within the user’s browser and only the user will have access to such data. Unfortunately, this may not be a viable option for public or work-related systems. Please note that deleting your browser history will remove all sleep data.
