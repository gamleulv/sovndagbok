Path to better sleep
Sleep Diary

In order to improve your sleep, it is important to first understand it. This sleep diary was designed to help Veterans track and review their sleep patterns. However, for it to be most beneficial to you, accurate information must be logged daily.

Sleep Diary use of local storage

The Sleep Diary uses a method called “local storage” to store and retrieve data. All data entered in this manner is securely stored within the user’s browser and only the user will have access to such data. Unfortunately, this may not be a viable option for public or work-related systems. For these instances please use the "Download Sleep Diary" link below to save it to your personal computer. Please note that deleting your browser history will remove all sleep data so it is best to periodically save your data by clicking the "Share Sleep Data" link on any page.
